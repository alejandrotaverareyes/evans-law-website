Brette L. Evans brings over 25 years of ongoing, uninterrupted legal experience — practicing since 1995 — with a primary focus on bankruptcy and family law. Over her career she has also practiced intellectual property and civil litigation.

She works directly with clients, experts, families, and businesses to navigate legal hurdles, and seeks to push the limits of the law for the best possible outcomes. Rather than taking the well-worn and easy path, Brette thrives on difficult cases — often working nights and weekends to get her clients the result they need.

Education

  • J.D., University of California, Hastings College of the Law
  • B.A. in Economics, University of California, Santa Cruz
  • Clerked for the Honorable James Lamden during law school

Beyond the office

Brette started her first business at sixteen, was a private pilot in Northern California in her twenties, and has spent time skydiving — the same drive she brings to her clients' cases.
