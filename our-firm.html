Our Firm

Evans Law Offices began representing clients in need of affordable family law and bankruptcy legal services in the Bay Area and other Northern California counties in 2000.

Who We Are

Experience, teamwork, and a fair fight

We focus on litigation, hearings, and trials — adversary proceedings, bankruptcy trials, and family matters including complex relocation, domestic violence, and custody disputes. We emphasize teamwork between attorneys, staff, and clients to achieve favorable outcomes while keeping legal help affordable. When a fair settlement is possible, we pursue it, working with opposing counsel and court personnel to minimize our clients' stress.
